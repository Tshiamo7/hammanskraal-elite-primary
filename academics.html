Our Approach

A Curriculum Built for Excellence

At Hammanskraal Elite School, we follow the CAPS (Curriculum and Assessment Policy Statement) curriculum as prescribed by the Department of Basic Education. Our dedicated teachers bring this curriculum to life through engaging lessons, hands-on activities, and personalized attention.

We believe in developing the whole child ‚Äì academically, socially, emotionally, and physically. Our balanced approach ensures that learners not only excel in their studies but also develop critical thinking skills, creativity, and a love for learning.

Small class sizes allow our teachers to provide individualized attention, ensuring that every learner receives the support they need to reach their full potential.
