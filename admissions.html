What You Need

Admission Requirements

Required Documents

  • Certified copy of learner's birth certificate
  • Certified copy of parent/guardian ID
  • Proof of residence (utility bill or lease agreement)
  • Previous school reports (if applicable)
  • Transfer card from previous school (if applicable)
  • Immunization records
  • Two recent passport-size photos

Age Requirements

  • Grade R: Turning 5 by 30 June of the year of admission
  • Grade 1: Turning 6 by 30 June of the year of admission
  • Grade 2-7: Age-appropriate placement based on previous schooling
  • Assessment may be required for placement
  • Late applications considered based on availability

Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the school year start?

Our school year follows the national school calendar, typically starting in mid-January and ending in early December. We have four terms with school holidays in between.

What are the school hours?

School hours are from 7:30 AM to 2:30 PM, Monday to Friday. The school office is open from 7:00 AM to 4:00 PM for enquiries and administrative matters.

Do you offer after-care services?

Yes, we offer after-care services until 5:00 PM for an additional fee. This includes supervised homework time, snacks, and recreational activities.

What is your learner-to-teacher ratio?

We maintain small class sizes with a maximum of 35 learners per class, ensuring each child receives adequate attention and support from our teachers.

Is there a school uniform?

Yes, all learners are required to wear the official school uniform. Details about the uniform and where to purchase it will be provided upon admission.

Need More Information?

Our admissions team is here to help you through the process. Contact us for any questions or to schedule a school visit.
