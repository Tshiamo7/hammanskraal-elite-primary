Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I schedule a school tour?

You can schedule a school tour by calling our office, sending an email, or filling out the contact form above. We typically respond within 24 hours to arrange a convenient time.

What documents do I need for admission?

You'll need the learner's birth certificate, parent/guardian ID, proof of residence, previous school reports (if applicable), and immunization records.

Do you offer transport services?

Currently, we do not provide transport services. However, many parents organize private transport groups. Contact us for more information.

Can I visit during school hours?

Yes, visitors are welcome during school hours. Please check in at the office upon arrival. For tours, we recommend scheduling in advance.
